      💬 WhatsApp Storage Rescue

      Step-by-step guides to recover storage from WhatsApp

      💾
      WhatsApp can use 5–15GB
      Videos, images, and status downloads accumulate silently
      🎬

      Delete Unnecessary Videos

      Open WhatsApp → Storage & Data → Manage Storage → Select videos over 5MB → Review & delete. Videos are the biggest storage consumers.

      📨

      Remove Forwarded Media

      In WhatsApp Manage Storage, filter by "Forwarded many times". These viral videos and images are usually safe to delete as you can always re-receive them.

      👁️

      Clean Status Downloads

      Status media saves to your phone automatically if auto-download is on. Check your WhatsApp folder: WhatsApp/Media/.Statuses in your file manager and remove old ones.

      📢

      Manage Channel & Group Media

      Leave high-traffic channels and groups that send lots of media. In each group: tap group name → Media, Links & Docs → clear what you don't need.

      ☁️

      Backup Chats Before Deleting

      Always go to Settings → Chats → Chat Backup → Back Up Now before clearing large amounts of media. This ensures your messages are safe on Google Drive.

      ⚙️

      Disable Auto-Download

      Settings → Storage & Data → Media auto-download. Set all three (Mobile, Wi-Fi, Roaming) to No media or Photos only. This prevents future storage buildup.

        🧹 Cache Cleaning Guides

        Device-specific step-by-step instructions

        ⚠️
        System cache cleaning procedures vary by device and Android version. Screenshots may differ from your exact device.
        📱
        SAMSUNG ONE UI
        Galaxy S / A / M / F Series
        1

        Open Settings

        Swipe down from top → Tap the ⚙️ gear icon, or find Settings in your app drawer.

        2

        Battery and Device Care

        Scroll down and tap "Battery and device care" (older: Device Maintenance).

        3

        Storage

        Tap the "Storage" option. Review what's using your space.

        4

        Apps

        Go back to Settings → Apps. Choose any app using large cache.

        5

        Clear Cache

        Inside the app: tap Storage → Clear Cache. Repeat for heavy apps like Chrome, Instagram, TikTok.

        📱
        XIAOMI MIUI / HyperOS
        Redmi / POCO / Mi Series
        1

        Open Phone Manager

        Find the "Phone Manager" or "Security" app on your home screen or drawer.

        2

        Cleaner / Clear Cache

        Tap "Cleaner" or "Clear Cache". MIUI will scan for junk files automatically.

        3

        Review & Confirm

        Review what will be deleted. Tap "Clean Up" to remove junk files safely.

        4

        Per-App Cache

        Settings → Apps → Manage Apps → select any app → Clear Data → Clear Cache.

        5

        Deep Clean

        In Phone Manager → Cleaner, tap "Deep Clean" for a thorough scan of large files.

        📱
        HUAWEI EMUI / HarmonyOS
        P / Mate / Nova Series
        1

        Open Phone Manager

        Open the "Phone Manager" app from your home screen.

        2

        Storage Cleaner

        Tap "Storage Cleaner" and let it scan your device for junk files.

        3

        Clean Up

        Review the scan results and tap "Clean Up" to safely remove cache and temporary files.

        4

        Per-App via Settings

        Settings → Apps → App List → select app → Storage → Clear Cache.

        📱
        VIVO FUNTOUCH OS / OriginOS
        V / Y / X / T Series
        1

        i-Manager

        Open "i-Manager" app or "iManager" from your home screen.

        2

        Memory Cleaner

        Tap "Memory Cleaner" or "Junk Cleaner" to start scanning.

        3

        One-Tap Clean

        Tap the clean button to clear junk files, cache, and temporary data.

        4

        Per-App

        Settings → More Settings → Applications → All Apps → select app → Clear Cache.

        📱
        OPPO ColorOS
        Reno / A / Find Series
        1

        Phone Manager

        Open "Phone Manager" from your home screen or app drawer.

        2

        Storage Space

        Tap "Storage Space" to see a breakdown of what's using storage.

        3

        Junk Files

        Tap "Clean Up" or "Junk Files". Review before tapping Clean.

        4

        Per-App Cache

        Settings → App Management → All Apps → select app → Storage Usage → Clear Cache.

        📱
        REALME UI
        Narzo / Number / C Series
        1

        Phone Manager

        Open "Phone Manager" or find the built-in cleaner on your home screen.

        2

        Clean & Boost

        Tap "Clean" and allow the scan to complete. This covers junk, cache, and temp files.

        3

        Confirm Cleaning

        Review the breakdown, uncheck anything you want to keep, then tap "Clean".

        4

        Per-App

        Settings → App Management → App List → select app → Usage & Storage → Clear Cache.

        🔋 Battery Care Center

        Expert guidance to extend your battery lifespan

        60%
        OPTIMAL CHARGE RANGE
        20% — 80%
        Staying in this range maximizes your battery's cycle count and long-term capacity.
        🔋 Battery Care Tips
        🌡️

        Avoid Extreme Heat

        Don't leave your phone in direct sunlight or hot cars. Heat above 35°C permanently degrades battery capacity faster than anything else.

        🔌

        Use Quality Chargers

        Use your manufacturer's original charger or a reputable brand. Cheap chargers deliver unstable voltages that damage the battery over time.

        🌙

        Avoid Overnight Charging

        Keeping your phone plugged in at 100% for hours generates heat and stress. Use battery limit features or charge before bed to 80%.

        📶

        Manage Background Apps

        Go to Settings → Battery → Background App Refresh and disable it for apps that don't need it. This reduces constant drain on your battery.

        ✈️

        Use Airplane Mode When Low Signal

        Your phone burns battery searching for signal in weak areas. Switch to Airplane Mode or Wi-Fi only when possible to conserve charge.

        🔆

        Lower Screen Brightness

        The display is your biggest power consumer. Use auto-brightness, reduce timeout to 30 seconds, and use Dark Mode — it saves significant battery on AMOLED screens.

        🔄

        Calibrate Monthly

        Once a month, let the battery drain to around 10%, then charge uninterrupted to 100%. This helps the battery management system read capacity accurately.
